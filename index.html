Since 2017, SENS-UAW has represented all academic student workers at The New School. This includes Course Assistants, Research Assistants and Associates, Teaching Assistants and Fellows, and Tutors. The union has been negotiating a new contract with the university after their original one expired on Aug. 31, 2023. After months of bargaining, SENS says TNS has not adequately responded to their demands. On March 6, after no agreement was reached, SENS went on strike.

The New School Free Press is committed to continuous coverage of negotiations and The New School community as they navigate the school's second strike in two years.
